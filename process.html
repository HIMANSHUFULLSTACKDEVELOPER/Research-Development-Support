Our Standards

Quality at Every Step

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a research paper?
Typically 7–15 days for a standard research paper, depending on the topic complexity and your specific requirements. Urgent requests with shorter deadlines can be accommodated at a nominal additional charge. We always confirm the timeline upfront before starting work.
Is my research kept confidential?
Absolutely. We maintain 100% confidentiality on all client projects. Your research topic, data, identity, and all communications are never shared with any third party. We sign NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements) on request for additional assurance.
Do you guarantee publication in Scopus/WoS journals?
We provide end-to-end publication support including journal selection, submission, and reviewer response. While final acceptance decisions lie with the journal editors, our expert guidance significantly increases your acceptance chances. We have a strong track record of successful publications.
Can I request changes after receiving the draft?
Yes! We offer unlimited revisions until you are completely satisfied with the work. Our goal is your 100% satisfaction. Simply share your feedback and we will revise promptly — no additional charges for revisions.
How do I make payment?
We offer flexible payment options including bank transfer, UPI, and other digital payment methods. Typically a nominal advance is taken to begin work, with the balance on delivery. We discuss payment terms transparently before starting any project.
Do you work with researchers outside Maharashtra?
Yes, we serve clients from across all 28 states of India and even international researchers. All communication and delivery is done digitally via WhatsApp, email, and video call — location is no barrier to getting our expert support.
