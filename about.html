Our Story

Built on Passion for Research

SIR Research Consultancy was founded in Nagpur with a single, powerful mission — to make world-class research support accessible to every scholar, student, and institution across India. What began as a small team of passionate PhD holders has grown into one of the most trusted names in academic research consultancy.

Over 15 years, we have helped over 5,000 clients get published in globally indexed journals, file hundreds of patents, complete their PhD theses with confidence, and achieve NAAC accreditation — all with unwavering commitment to quality and confidentiality.

Today, our team of 40+ domain experts, writers, statisticians, and IPR professionals operates round-the-clock to deliver excellence — from Nagpur to the world.
