Step-by-Step Process

Our Research Process

A transparent, structured workflow that ensures publication-quality output at every stage

1

Idea Discussion & Topic Finalization

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your domain, research goals, and journal targets. Together we finalize a novel, publishable topic that fills an existing research gap.

Day 1–3
2

Research & Literature Review

Our team conducts an extensive survey of existing literature using Scopus, Google Scholar, and IEEE Xplore. We identify key papers, extract gaps, and structure a comprehensive review section.

Week 1–2
3

Methodology & Implementation

We design a robust research methodology — algorithms, experimental setup, dataset selection, simulation, or survey instruments — and implement the proposed approach with proper documentation.

Week 3–6
4

Results & Analysis

Statistical tools (SPSS, MATLAB, Python, R) are used to derive results. Charts, tables, and comparative analysis are prepared with meaningful interpretations aligned with research objectives.

Week 7–8
5

Draft Writing

The complete paper is drafted following the target journal's author guidelines — including abstract, keywords, all sections, references (IEEE/APA/Elsevier style), and visual formatting.

Week 9–11
6

Review & Editing

You review the full draft. We incorporate all your feedback and apply expert language editing for grammar, clarity, flow, and technical accuracy. Unlimited revisions until you're satisfied.

Week 12
7

Plagiarism Check

Final paper is run through Turnitin / iThenticate. If similarity exceeds 10%, we rewrite and paraphrase to bring it within acceptable limits. You receive the official plagiarism report.

Week 12–13
8

Journal Selection

We select the most appropriate Scopus / WoS indexed journal based on your topic, impact factor requirement, acceptance rate, processing time, and APC budget.

Week 13
9

Paper Submission

We handle the complete submission process — cover letter preparation, author registration on journal portals, uploading files, and confirming receipt from the editorial team.

Week 14
10

Reviewer Comments Handling

When reviewers respond, we prepare a point-by-point rebuttal letter and revise the paper accordingly — significantly increasing the chance of final acceptance. Full support until publication.
